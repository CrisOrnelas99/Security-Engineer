# What I Learned — Governance, Regulation & Compliance

- **Governance** = directing and managing an organisation to meet objectives and comply with laws/standards.
- **Regulation** = laws/rules enforced by authorities to protect against harm.
- **Compliance** = adhering to applicable laws, regulations, and standards.

---

## Information Security Governance

- Framework of **structures, policies, processes, and guidelines** to ensure:
  - Confidentiality
  - Integrity
  - Availability (CIA) of information assets.
- Overseen by **top management**.
- Essential for:
  - Risk management
  - Protecting sensitive data
  - Regulatory compliance

### Core Governance Processes:
- **Strategy**: Align security strategy with business objectives.
- **Policies & Procedures**: Define how information assets are used and protected.
- **Risk Management**: Identify threats and implement mitigation controls.
- **Performance Measurement**: Use KPIs/metrics to evaluate security effectiveness.
- **Compliance**: Ensure adherence to regulations and best practices.

---

## Information Security Regulation

- Legal and regulatory frameworks that mandate data protection.
- Designed to prevent:
  - Unauthorised access
  - Data theft
  - Data misuse
- Compliance is typically **mandatory** and enforced by authorities.

### Examples:
- **GDPR** → Data privacy (EU citizens).
- **HIPAA** → Healthcare data protection (US).
- **PCI-DSS** → Payment card data security.
- **GLBA** → Protection of financial customer information.

---

## Benefits of Governance & Regulation

- Stronger security posture.
- Reduced risk of breaches.
- Increased stakeholder trust.
- Avoidance of legal/financial penalties.
- Better alignment with business goals.
- Improved risk-based decision-making.
- Competitive advantage through demonstrated security maturity.

---

## Key Takeaway

- Governance defines **how security is managed**.
- Regulation defines **what must legally be followed**.
- Compliance proves **adherence to required standards**.
- Together, they create a structured, accountable, and secure organisational environment.
